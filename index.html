Q&A

  1. Three main diffrences between web1, web2 and web3

    1. Web1 was primarily gave information in the form of home pages, but in web2 (Blogs / Wikis) and web3 (Live-streams / Waves) the web has become increasingly interactive, and its mostly the user that produce the content.
    2. In Web 3.0, computers can interpret information like humans and intelligently generate and distribute useful content tailored to the needs of users. that called recommendation systems.
    3. In web3, The three-dimensional design is being used widely. Museum guides, computer games, ecommerce, geospatial contexts, etc. are all examples that use 3D graphics.

  2. The diffrences as represented in the Use Case of medicines side effects:

    • In Web1: the user would have to write the spevific path for the website that he wanted to read from the side effects of a certain medicine.
    • In web2: the user can search in search engines a specific question about a specific medicine and discover his side effects and get back a webpage about it.
    • In web3: the user can search in search engines a specific question about a specific medicine and discover his side effects and get back a specific part in a webpage that specifily tells about it.
